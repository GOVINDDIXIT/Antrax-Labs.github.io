MODE joining IDTechEx Show! in Santa Clara

Posted November 06, 2017

On November 15 and 16, MODE will be at the IDTechEx Show! in Santa Clara, California. As part of the Sensors USA event, we will be showcasing some of our latest work. Come by booth T37 to check out our Sensor Cloud solution and the Evaluation Kit. You will also see demos of our Vehicle Research Cloud, as well as our newest product – Factory Data Cloud.

The IDTechEx Show! presents the latest emerging technologies at one event, with nine concurrent technologies and a single exhibition covering 3D Printing, Electric Vehicles, Energy Harvesting, Energy Storage, Graphene, Internet of Things, Printed Electronics, Sensors & Wearable Technology.
