Webinar: Key Technical Decisions for IoT

Posted October 03, 2016

For the next installment of our webinar series, we will talk about our favorite topic — software for IoT! We’ll discuss setting expectations and going through some important technical decisions such as connectivity and protocols.

We will wrap up with a short Q&A. Feel free to send your questions to info@tinkermode.com and we will be sure to answer them.

Date
Tuesday, October 11th, 2016

Time
11:00 - 11:45 AM Pacific Time

Speaker
Gaku Ueda
CEO, MODE, Inc.

Level
Beginner/intermediate

Who should attend?
Anyone building an IoT product. We will be introducing important technical decisions and how to approach them, so some background knowledge is encouraged.

Agenda

1. Setting Production Definition

2. Demystifying Connectivity Choices
