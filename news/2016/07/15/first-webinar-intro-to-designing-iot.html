Announcing Our First Webinar: Introduction to Designing IoT Products for Enterprises

Posted July 15, 2016

We are excited to announce the first installment of the MODE Webinar Series: “Introduction to Designing IoT Products for Enterprises.” In this webinar, we will discuss what it takes to turn your existing product into an IoT product.

Date
Wednesday, August 3rd, 2016

Time
11:00 AM Pacific Time

Speaker
Gaku Ueda
CEO, MODE, Inc.

Level
Beginner

Who should attend?
Anyone interested in building an IoT product! We will focus on enterprises looking to scale production into the thousands, but makers/hobbyists are welcome as well.



Register Now


Agenda

1. Why should you develop IoT products?

IoT isn’t just for making smart home devices that can be remotely controlled. For your company, the full potential of IoT can help bring all kinds of business opportunities and cost savings. We’ll talk about the benefits of creating smart, connected products first.

2. What are the essential components for an IoT product?

We’ll talk about the fundamentals of IoT, and all the different software components that go into making a smart product.

What exactly do you need to connect a hardware product? What is an IoT platform? What is a gateway? How do you connect Bluetooth devices or other mesh network devices? How do you build an app that enables remote control? How will your customers see their data?

3. What are the challenges?

Developing and operating an IoT product requires you to make certain considerations. We will cover important aspects such as IoT product security, reliability, and scalability.

4. Q&A

Have questions of your own? Submit questions throughout the webinar and we’ll answer as many as we can!
