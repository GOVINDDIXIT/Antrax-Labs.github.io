MODE Vehicle Research Cloud

The MODE Vehicle Research Cloud is a ready-to-use solution for collecting and managing sensor data remotely. It supports all kinds of sensors from various sensor manufacturers, including your company’s own custom sensors.

Vehicle Research Cloud Features

The MODE Vehicle Research Cloud offers a robust and secure set of features to professionals seeking to access their data. Check out a few of the ways we’re different from our competition.

  • Multi-Vehicle Coordination

    You can facilitate a data collection session that involves multiple vehicles.

  • High-Frequency Data

    R&D projects demand data collection at high frequency. Unlike many fleet management solutions, MODE’s Vehicle Research Cloud allows you to collect data from vehicles at intervals as small as milliseconds.

  • Collect Additional Data

    MODE VRC is built on top of MODE’s Sensor Cloud technology. You can collect data from additional sensors, such as vehicle interior and freight environment sensors, and driver biometric sensors.

  • In-Vehicle Gateway

    MODE offers an in-vehicle gateway that you can simply plug into the 12V DC power source in a vehicle and start sending data to the cloud via 3G/LTE connection. You can literally connect a vehicle to the cloud in 5 minutes.

  • Unlimited Expandability

    MODE’s Vehicle Research Cloud is just the starting point of your cloud vehicle management. You can easily expand and customize our solution to meet your unique vehicle management needs.

  • Third Party Integration

    MODE VRC is your first step in implementing AI analysis of vehicle data. The data collected can be exported to any third-party cloud services specializing in big-data analysis and machine learning.
