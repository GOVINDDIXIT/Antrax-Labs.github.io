The easiest and most comprehensive solution for IoT developers

Creating a real-world system that connects hardware devices requires a lot more than just device connectivity, which is what many IoT platforms only provide.

You need to handle users and authentication. You need to handle device provisioning and WiFi setup. And how do you keep your system up and running 24/7?

Typical IoT platforms only cover a small part of your IoT development. A huge amount of custom software needs to be built before you can even start working on the core of what you want to accomplish.

Antrax Lab is changing that.

Here’s how we compare with the competition

Antrax’s goal is to free you from designing and developing the foundation, and let you focus on what matters to your company.
